FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

  • A camel is an even-toed ungulate in the genus Camelus that bears distinctive fatty deposits known as "humps"
  • on its back. Camels have long been domesticated and, as livestock, they provide food and textiles.

FACTS ABOUT PANDA

  • The giant panda, also known as the panda bear or simply the panda, is a bear native to South Central China.
  • It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name "giant panda" is sometimes
  • used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring musteloid.
